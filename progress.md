## How This Works

- The **Archmage** issues **one move at a time** — a single next step (watch this lecture / do this pset / summon this structure), never the whole rank at once.
- Before every move, the Archmage **convenes the Council**: the advisor agents audit the candidate move against their domains (learning science, course audit, the bootcamp frame, the project bank, the Tower lore).
- You **report back** when a move is done ("done", "I finished X"), and the Archmage consults the Council again for the next one.
- **Update this file after each step:** add the completed work to the Mastery Ledger (with its mastery signal), advance the Current Rank when a Gate is passed, and rewrite the Next Candidate Move.
- You advance by **passing a Gate, never by time served.** A plateau triggers a deload, not grinding. A failed Gate sends you back to drill.
